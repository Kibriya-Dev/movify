Spider-Man is a beloved superhero film that follows the story of Peter Parker, a high school student who gains extraordinary powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider. As he struggles with personal loss and moral dilemmas, Peter learns that "with great power comes great responsibility."

The movie delivers a perfect mix of action, heart, and humor. Whether it’s Tobey Maguire’s emotional portrayal, Andrew Garfield’s charm, or Tom Holland’s youthful energy — each Spider-Man version brings something unique to the character. The visual effects, iconic villains like Green Goblin and Doc Ock, and the relatable story of growing up make it a timeless superhero classic.
